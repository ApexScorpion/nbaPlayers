Description

Stephen Curry plays for the Golden state warriors. He is highly regarded as the best shooter of all time. He also holds the record for the most 3 pointers made. Steph Curry is a great ball-handler and also excels at creating shots for himself. He is also a 4 time NBA champion, 2 time MVP, and a 9 time NBA All-Star.
