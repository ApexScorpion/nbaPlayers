Description

Trae Young is an NBA athlete who plays for the Atlanta Hawks. He was a NCAA superstar, he was the only player to lead in points and assists in a single season. He was drafter in 2018 to Dallas but was traded to Atlanta. He is a 2 time NBA All-Star and he is a very skilled player. Trae Young excels in creating shots for himself while also being an elite playmaker.
